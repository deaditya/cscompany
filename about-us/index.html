Ceo Greetings

Welcome to CS Company. Thank you for having interest in CS COMPANY.

 

CS COMPANY is opening a new future of the contents of South Korea by informing Korea’s culture, art, education, etc. and by promoting Korea to all over the world.

It became 11th anniversary since CS Company was founded in 2009. Today, CS COMPANY grew as a key player of a Korean culture reminder because of your effort to love the country and cherish our own cultures indeed.

In order to remunerate, CS COMPANY is continue working to improve the status of South Korea by promoting our variety sectors as in culture, art, education, etc.

In addition, CS COMPANY is a professional company in performing Concert and event planning, exhibitions, videos, visual, stage design, cultural arts program development, cultural fusion educational services, public relations, planning, etc. over the years. We serve as an agent to deal with international projects as well as domestic projects based on accumulated skills and know-how from number of years in business.

CS COMPANY will do our best to make the cultural contents that can sympathize with customers, corporations that are working with CS COMPANY, and those who love Korea.

At last, we hope full of happiness and blessing in every home. Thank you.
